Important Questions

  • Explain the principles of classical mechanics and their applications in everyday life.
  • Discuss the concepts of electric fields and forces, and their significance in understanding electrical phenomena.
  • Describe the behavior of light using geometric and wave optics principles.
  • What are the key postulates of special relativity, and how do they affect our understanding of space and time?
  • Discuss the wave-particle duality of matter and energy in the context of quantum mechanics.
  • Explain the structure of the atom and the process of nuclear decay.
  • How do elementary particles contribute to our understanding of the fundamental forces of nature?
  • Provide examples of applications of physics concepts in engineering and technology.
  • Discuss the role of physics in addressing contemporary challenges such as climate change and sustainable energy.
  • What are some emerging areas of research in physics, and how might they impact future technologies?
